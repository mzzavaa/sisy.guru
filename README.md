# SISY.guru - Mentoring Marketplace for Women Experts

Live site: https://sisy.guru

Part of the [SISY ecosystem](https://sisy.world) - visibility infrastructure for women experts.

## About

SISY.guru is the mentoring marketplace where women experts learn from women who have been there. Book sessions, follow mentorship tracks, and grow your expert visibility.

## SISY Ecosystem

| Site | Purpose |
|------|---------|

| [sisy.my](https://sisy.my) | Expert profiles |

## Pricing Model

Mentors set their own rates. SISY takes a 15-20% platform commission.
